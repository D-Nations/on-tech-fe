About us

ON Tech aims to be a development partner that pairs genuine technical depth with communication that meets people where they are.

Mission

Making technology more approachable.

"We want to use AI, but we don't know where to start" — answering that is exactly our role. We deliver difficult technology through clear language and dependable implementation.

The strength of a focused team

In large organizations, decisions and responses can drag. Because ON Tech is a focused team, we engage deeply with each client and respond quickly and flexibly. The same people handle everything from first contact to development, so nothing gets lost in translation.

Beyond the language barrier

Attentive communication in Japanese, and international collaboration in English. Being fluent in both is a defining strength. Domestic clients and globally minded ones alike can hand work to us with confidence.

Values

What we hold to

Integrity

We're honest about what we can and can't do. No overpromising — just value, delivered reliably.

Clarity

We talk in your words, not jargon. Moving forward with full understanding matters most to us.

Lasting quality

No quick fixes — design built for operation. Systems you can rely on, for the long run.
