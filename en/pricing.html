Pricing

We offer two engagement models to fit how you want to work. We'll recommend the best fit for the scale and goals of your project.

Recommended

Retainer

Monthly / ongoing support

For those who want to keep developing and improving. Like having an in-house dev team, available month to month for the technical support you need.

  • Ongoing AI agent & system development
  • Operation, maintenance, improvement proposals
  • Technical advice & sounding board
  • Priority response
  • Japanese & English support

Spot development

Per project / quote on request

For those with a clear "I want to build this." We quote up front based on requirements, then develop and deliver per project.

  • AI agent & tool development
  • Backend / API development
  • Data analytics & infrastructure
  • End-to-end from requirements definition

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Will you take on small requests?

Yes. We welcome starting small as a trial. Please feel free to reach out.

Retainer or spot — which is better?

If ongoing improvement is expected, a retainer fits best; for a clearly scoped one-off, spot development. If you're unsure, we'll advise during our conversation.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfer. Details are provided at contract time.

Can you work with remote clients?

Yes — we work remotely across all of Japan, with meetings held online.
